About The Orana Hotels And Resort

Setting the stage for a restful stay, their luxury guest rooms are some of the largest in Delhi NCR. Their hotel is a partner of Friendshiptime.com pvt ltd. Sleep soundly in hotel's lavish featherbeds outfitted with crisp linens and down comforters. The spacious work areas and high-speed Wi-Fi Internet facilitate business traveller's needs. A unique combination of warm ambiance, exotic location and top notch service is what truly defines Orana Hotels and Resorts. Designed to offer an invigorating and relaxing experience, this luxury resort in Delhi is counted among the top hotels in Delhi and has raised the bar of comfort and lavishness to an altogether new level. Located four kilometers from Delhi Airport, the hotel offers easy access to the Delhi Metro and NH8. Offering convenient access to the business district of Gurgaon and tourist attractions in South and Central Delhi, the hotel is ideal for business and leisure travellers. Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi and complimentary transfers to airport as per the package. Sprawling across eight acres of land, the luxurious resort provides several opportunities for visitors including couples, friends and family to enjoy the unmatched services of this luxury resort in New Delhi and make their holidays memorable.
