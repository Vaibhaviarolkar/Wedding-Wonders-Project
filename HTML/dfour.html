About Happy Shades Event Company

Elements Decor are the wedding specialists that design and supervise the construction and production of event such as weddings, themed parties, anniversaries, etc. This Delhi-based wedding design & planning company was founded in the year 2010 and since then, it has a proven track record of delivering high-quality unique event solutions to its clients. The Elements Decor team loves "all things wedding" and because of that they have always been able to live up to their objective, i.e., "Client families enjoy their big day, without having to get into the nitty-gritty and prep work."
