About Udman By Ferns And Petals

Udman, a Boutique Resort by Ferns N Petals is the first banqueting property on National Highway-8. The venue comprises of an air-conditioned banquet and a pool-side lawn with a glasshouse to add a European feel. The resort?s exterior facade is surrounded by lush landscaping and has a dedicated passage for the grooms? convoy.
